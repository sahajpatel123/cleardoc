## 2. Problem Statement
- Legal documents are intentionally confusing and intimidating
- People face eviction notices, insurance denials, medical bills, IRS letters, etc.
- Most can't afford lawyers to decipher these documents
- Consequences of misunderstanding: lost rights, money, housing, status

## 3. Solution
- ClearDoc: AI-powered document analyzer that provides:
  - Plain English explanations of legal jargon
  - Identification of red flags (illegal demands, bluffs, threats)
  - Generated counter-letters citing relevant statutes
  - Concrete next steps ranked by effectiveness
- Process: Upload document → optional context → get analysis in ~30 seconds
- Privacy: Original files not stored, only results saved; encrypted in transit

## 4. Product Demo / How It Works
- Three simple steps:
  1. Upload (PDF, PNG, JPG up to 10MB)
  2. Whisper context (optional one-line situation description)
  3. Walk back armed (receive plain English, red flags, counter-letter, next steps)
- Live demo: Document reader with real-time annotation as you scroll
- Supported document types: Insurance denials, eviction notices, medical bills, IRS letters, visa rejections, debt collection, landlord ultimatums, bank disputes, contract traps, consumer rights, billing errors, legal threats

## 5. Key Features
- **Plain English Translation**: Every clause explained like a friend at your kitchen table
- **Red Flag Detection**: Illegal demands, manipulation, and bluffs flagged with exact triggering sentences
- **Counter-Letter Generation**: Firm, formal reply quoting relevant statutes back at them
- **Actionable Next Steps**: 3-5 concrete actions ranked by likelihood of working
- **Free Tier**: First analysis free, no card required
- **Pro Subscription**: $9/month for unlimited analyses, follow-up capabilities
- **Privacy-First**: Files not stored, only results saved; end-to-end encryption

## 6. Market Opportunity
- TAM: Anyone facing legal/administrative documents in the US (~100M+ potential users annually)
- SAM: Individuals dealing with specific document types (eviction, insurance, medical billing, immigration, etc.)
- SOM: Initial target: 1% of SAM within first year
- Growth drivers: Increasing legal complexity, pro se litigation rise, distrust of institutions

## 7. Business Model
- Freemium: Free analysis per day (limited)
- Pro Subscription: $9/month billed annually ($108/year) or $12/month monthly
- Team/Enterprise plans: Volume pricing for organizations helping clients (legal aid, tenant unions, etc.)
- No hidden fees: Results only, no data selling

## 8. Traction / Validation

## 9. Go-to-Market Strategy
- Content marketing: Blog posts explaining common legal documents
- Partnerships: Tenant unions, consumer advocacy groups, immigration nonprofits
- SEO: Target long-tail keywords like "what does this eviction notice mean"
- Social media: Short videos showing before/after document analysis
- Referral program: Free month for both referrer and referee

## 10. Competitive Landscape
- **Alternatives**: Lawyers ($150-$500/hr), legal document services (expensive, slow), DIY (risky, time-consuming)
- **ClearDoc Advantages**: 
  - Instant (30 seconds vs days/weeks)
  - Affordable ($9/mo vs hundreds)
  - Educational (teaches users to spot issues themselves)
  - Private (no file storage)
  - Engaging UI/UX reduces anxiety
